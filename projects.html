Blackboard-An Online Student Portal

1 2 3 4 5

Blackboard, is an online student portal with the aim of helping students through their trials and travails of online education.
It provides features such as verifying email on sign up through verification code, book barter system, management of To-Do lists, providing Previous Year Questions, management of time table and class links, and a social media platform with limited interaction.
The project used Django, MySql, and basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
